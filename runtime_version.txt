Packaging checklist:
1. Keep runtime/python, runtime/ffmpeg/bin, and runtime/wheels in the package.
2. Do not package runtime/venv; setup_runtime.ps1 recreates it from local wheels.
3. Confirm runtime/python/Lib/venv/__init__.py exists.
4. Confirm runtime/wheels/tkinterdnd2-0.4.3-py3-none-any.whl exists.
5. Clean logs, output, temp, runtime/venv, .transkun_runtime_ok, __pycache__, and *.pyc.
6. Use 7-Zip to create Transkun-Windows-OneClick.7z; do not use PowerShell Compress-Archive.
